Senior FP&A Professional

I am a FP&A Professiona who loves giving data-driven solutions with smart tech.
My foundation is as an FP&A Professional of 7+years.
I learned the ropes of Excel reporting and budgeting automation in my former years (first 3 years).
These are the years I honed financial modelling.
After this, my manager and mentor at BCA LOGISTICS LIMITED introduced me to the world of BI. I learned R. Ever since then, I switched gears and led FP&A through a digital transformation.
Today, I partner with leadership and help drive the business strategy.
I do this by offering data-driven solutions. PowerBI is one of the ways to present those solutions.

My Finance tech stack evolved from Excel in the first year of work, to now include VBA, Power Query, PowerBI (inc. DAX), SQL, COGNOS, TM1, R.
My favourite is Power Query (PQ). One of the biggest benefits of learning PQ is actually how it reshapes your mind. It empowers you to think hard about how to structure data, and how to model it.

I am also a writer FP&A Trends Group. I have 2 articles coming out in the next 2 months. keep an eye out! :)
